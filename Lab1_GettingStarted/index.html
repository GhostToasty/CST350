Pollution Patrol

Explore the deep sea and save the ocean from the perils of pollution. Learn about pollution, clean up the ocean, and make new sea friends along the way! Collect all your findings in the Scuba Scout Guide!

A downwards scroll point-and-click game where kids can explore the sea as a Scuba Scout. An educational web game where kids will not only learn about sea life but the affects of pollution in the ocean, inspiring them to take better care of the planet.
